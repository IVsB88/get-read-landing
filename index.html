Features

Simple, intuitive, and designed to help you manage your reading list effectively

  • Save Links with a Simple Message

    Just send any URL to the bot, and it's saved for later reading. No more forgetting interesting articles or resources.

  • Schedule Reminders Your Way

    Choose when to be reminded: tomorrow, in 2 days, or in 3 days. If you don't select, we'll automatically remind you at 9 AM tomorrow.

  • Not Ready to Read? Just Snooze

    When you receive a reminder but don't have time, simply hit "Remind me tomorrow" and we'll get back to you.

How It Works

Using Read Complete Bot is simple and straightforward

  • 1 Send a link
  • 2 Choose when to be reminded
  • 3 Get your reminder
  • 4 Read the content

Start the bot by searching for @your_bot_username on Telegram or clicking the button below. Then, simply send any link you want to save. The bot will ask when you'd like to be reminded. When you receive a reminder, you can either read the content or snooze it for later.
