Features

  • Quick Link Saving

  • Smart Reminders

  • Organized Reading List

How It Works

Using GetRead! Bot is simple and intuitive

  • 1 Send a Link
  • 2 Set Reminder
  • 3 Get Notified
  • 4 Read Content

The bot saves any link you send. Choose when you want to be reminded, or use our default 9 AM reminder. When it's time, you'll receive a notification with your saved link, ready for reading!
